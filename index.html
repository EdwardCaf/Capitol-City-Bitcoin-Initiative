Our Vision

We see a future where Concord becomes the Bitcoin hub of the state. Individuals, businesses, and local government, all can benefit by adopting Bitcoin.

Adopting Bitcoin

  • Accept Bitcoin for payment of goods and services
  • Spend Bitcoin at local businesses
  • Boosts local economy and tax revenue
  • Inclusive financial system, only need a phone to participate in the local economy

Bitcoin as Savings
  • Bitcoin cannot be inflated or debased
  • Retaining/Increasing purchasing power over long term vs Dollars
Bitcoin as Money
  • Instant and final settlement
  • 24/7/365 uptime
  • Easy to exchange between Dollars and Bitcoin
  • Control and custody over your money, like cash



As a Mentor, my mission is to help people along their Bitcoin journey. I can help with everything from self-custody, to running a node, and the cutting edge of Bitcoin. You can email me and book a free 1-on-1 session to learn more!
